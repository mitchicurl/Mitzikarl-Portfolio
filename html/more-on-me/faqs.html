Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about myself.

What course you are taking at college?
I am currently a first-year student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at the Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila (PLM).
Why did you choose this course?
Since when I was 10 years old, I have been fascinated by technology, such as mobile devices and computers. I chose Computer Science because it allows me to combine my passion for problem-solving, creativity, and innovation. I believe this field offers endless opportunities to make a positive impact on society through technology.
Do you join any clubs or organizations?
Yes! I had registered as multimedia junior officer in the student council of College of Information Systems and Technology Management (CISTM) in PLM. Currently, I am at the stage of interviewing process to be part of them.
What are your hobbies or interests outside school?
My hobbies include playing video games, listening to music, watching movies, and photography. I also enjoy coding personal projects and contributing to open-source communities in my free time.
Do you have a sports or fitness routine?
I am a fan of football and basketball, but I don't play them regularly. However, I do try to stay active by going for walks and doing home workouts.
What is your biggest goal as a student?
My biggest goal as a student is to continually grow not only academically but also personally. I want to develop strong critical thinking, communication, and problem-solving skills that will help me succeed beyond school. I also aim to graduate with latin honors for myself and my family.
What are your future plans after graduation?
After graduation, I want to continue learning about emerging fields like artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity to stay updated in the tech industry. In the long term, I hope to contribute to meaningful projects, either by working in a leading tech company or building my own startup, where I can apply my skills to make technology more accessible and impactful.
What’s something unique about you?
One unique thing about me is my ability to quickly adapt to new environments and challenges. Whether it's learning a new programming language or adjusting to a different culture, I thrive on change and see it as an opportunity for growth. This adaptability has helped me navigate various situations in both my academic and personal life effectively.
